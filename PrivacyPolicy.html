Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect when you use our mobile app product ("PickedMail”) and how we use this information. This policy serves as an addition to the App Store End User License Agreement. The information PickedMail gathers is used solely for purposes of providing better service to you. If the content of our service changes, we would update our Privacy Policy. We do not loan, rent or sell information to others, other than as stated herein. We are committed to protect your privacy. We collect two types of information through the PickedMail app: General Statistical Information and Personal Information.

General Statistical Information

When you use the PickedMail app we gather statistics and analytics on your usage of the app through Crashlytics. We may collect information about your mobile device and internet connection, including the application-specific device identifier, IP address, operating system, browser type, mobile network information, location, installed email etc. This information is collectively referred to as "General Statistical Information". This data helps us to determine how different parts of the PickedMail app are used. This information is used by us to provide you with the best user experience possible.

Personal Information

In addition to General Statistical Information, we also collect personally information. Information that can reasonably be used to identify you and your personal traits is referred to as "Personal Information". The first email address you add to Picked.email is used as your username. We might use that email address to reach out to you periodically with information about features, updates, announcements or to request your feedback. After a user register and grant permission, Picked.email server may download and store the full content of user's emails. Only plain text information and HTML text (excluding attachments) will be processed by the Picked.email server, in order to provide email service that refers to your personal need. The email information stored on the server will be unanonymous in order to provide personal classification to your email. However, the information will not be directly accessed without your approval.

Data Security

Accounts are added to Picked.email through OAuth where possible. OAuth is an industry standard for authorizing secure delegated access to external apps and service providers. When connecting Trove via OAuth, we never receive or store your password and you can revoke our access at anytime.

We use Vultr’s cloud infrastructure to store your data. Apart from the Vultr' security policies, we take a number of measures to ensure that your data is never read by anyone else. We ensure that all data transmissions are secured using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption. The safety and security of your information also depends on you. You should not share your email user name and password with anyone. If you find out that anyone has improperly obtained your login credentials and accesses your email account through PickedMail, you should immediately contact the administrator. We are not responsible for such unauthorized access.
